Welcome to christmas.origami-kids.com Here we have collected together some fun origami Christmas models to enjoy with your kids. You can use them to decorate your house, your parcels, or your tree - or you can make Christmas cards and gift tags, fridge magnets, bookmarks ... we are sure you can think of some more good ideas!

And the best thing about origami christmas decorations and gifts is they are so cheap to make! Most of the models in the pictures below are made with scraps of gift paper, and leftover origami paper... and yet they look so lovely!

On this page you will find all of these wonderful origami models to make and share, just scroll down to browse through!
